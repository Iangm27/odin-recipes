Pizza

Description

This grilled pizza recipe uses pizza dough made from scratch, topped with mozzarella cheese, sauce, and your choice of savory toppings. Grilled to perfection for a fun outdoor treat.

Ingredients

  • 3 ½ cups all-purpose flour, or as needed
  • 1 envelope Fleischmann's Pizza Crust Yeast
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder1 ⅓ cups warm water (120 to 130 degrees F/50 degrees C)
  • ⅓ cup oil, more as needed
  • ½ cup pizza sauce, or to taste
  • ½ cup shredded mozzarella cheese, or to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  2. Combine 2 cups flour, yeast, sugar, and salt in a large bowl. Add warm water and oil; mix until well combined, about 1 minute. Gradually add enough flour to form a slightly sticky dough ball. Knead on a floured work surface until dough is smooth, elastic, and not sticky, about 5 minutes.
  3. Divide dough into 8 equal portions. Pat or roll dough on a floured work surface to about 8-inch circles (they don't need to be perfect).
  4. Brush both sides of crusts with additional oil; place on the grill. Cook until bottoms are lightly browned and tops look set, 3 to 4 minutes. Remove crusts from grill with long-handled tongs; place on a baking sheet, grilled-side up.
  5. Spread a light coating of sauce over each crust. Top with cheese.
  6. Slide pizzas back on the grill. Cook until bottom crusts are brown and cheese melts, 3 to 4 minutes. Serve immediately.
